Cognitive Dissonance

If you clicked Yes...

You demonstrated avoidant attachment. You were already betrayed by one other group and when asked to give up your food you accepted. You disregarded your own struggles and needs in order to maintain peace.

If you clicked No...

You demonstrated social-exchange theory. You knew that joining the group would mean giving up your food so you knew that you would ultimately lose in this transaction. Thus you rejected the offer.

Avoidant Attachment

Attachments marked by discomfort over, or resistance to, being close to others. An insecure attachment style

Social-Exchange Theory

The theory that human interactions are transactions that aim to maximize one's rewards and minimize one's costs.
