You demonstrated in group bias and mirror-image perception. You sided with people that you shared a community with and chose to see the other negatively.

In Group Bias

Unjustified negative behavior toward a group or its members.

Mirror-Image Perceptions

Reciprocal views of each other often held by parties in conflict; for example, each may view itself as moral and peace-loving and the other as evil and aggressive.
